About Me

Thomas is a fledgling Full Stack Web Developer with a background in Philosophy, French and Music Production. A teacher and student both professionally and as a hobby, he finds learning and teaching to be mirror images of what it means to have a deep engagement with knowledge and information. H excels in abstract thinking and synthesizing ideas from disparate disciplines into effective solutions, regardless of obstacles. Friendly and personable, he is capable of boosting morale or creating an environment for critical analysis as needed. It is also possible that he is a cat.
